# Controlled GEO monitoring protocol

The private evidence workbook contains the full prompt-by-surface matrix. Store screenshots and notes only in the private `Medical Evidence/Monitoring` folders, never in this repository or on the public site.

Run the matrix immediately before release and at days 30, 60, and 90. Use fresh, signed-out or non-personalized sessions where the product permits it. Record when a surface is unavailable; do not substitute another product silently.

For Google Search AI features, ChatGPT Search, Microsoft Copilot, and Perplexity, record:

- test date, surface availability, and exact Greek prompt;
- whether the doctor/site is mentioned;
- direct links and cited sources;
- whether the medical description is accurate, partly accurate, concerning, or incorrect;
- a screenshot and concise notes, including any regional or session limitation.

The matrix covers brand, Thessaloniki-local, treatment, reconstructive, and diagnostic “best surgeon” intents. “Best” prompts are diagnostics only. Their results must not be converted into superiority, ranking, guarantee, or testimonial language.

Success is directional: correct indexing, useful non-brand impressions and queries in Search Console, and more accurate AI mentions. There is no guaranteed ranking, citation rate, or deadline. Escalate medically concerning output for review; do not rewrite the site merely to mirror an AI answer.
